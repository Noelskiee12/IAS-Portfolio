AIS NOTES

IMPORTANCE OF IT SECURITY

Information assurance practice of assuring information and managing risks related to the use, processing, storage, and transmission of information or data, including protection of the integrity, availability, authenticity, non-repudiation and confidentiality of user data.

VIOLATION OF SECURITY PRINCIPLE

CIA

— is a guiding model in information security. A comprehensive information security strategy includes policies and security controls that minimize threats to these three crucial components.

Confidentiality

— Keeping information secret and accessible only to authorized people.

Integrity

— Keeping information accurate and unchanged unless modified by authorized users.

Availability

— Making sure information and systems are accessible when needed.

CAREER OPPORTUNITIES IN IT SECURITY

WHAT IS CYBERSECURITY

— Practice of protecting computer systems, networks, and sensitive data from theft, damage, or unauthorized access.

Red vs Blue Team

Red — They simulate real-world cyberattacks to find weaknesses in systems. (offense)

Blue — They detect, respond to, and protect against attacks from the Red Team. (defense)

Security Operational Center

— Monitors and defends systems from cyber threats.

Three Pillars of Cybersecurity

  • People are crucial for strong cybersecurity.
  • Processes define security procedures and responses.
  • Technology provides tools to protect systems.

All three pillars are essential for effective defense.

Vulnerability Scanning

— Automatically finds weaknesses in systems.

Penetration Testing

— Tries to exploit those weaknesses to see how far an attacker could go.
